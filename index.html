Rules / Piece Movement

Tap a piece to highlight legal moves. Green means a legal move, red means capture, and purple means a drop square.

Victory: capture the enemy Core, checkmate it, encircle a checked Core with no escape squares, or completely block the enemy.

Pieces:
★ Core: moves 1 square along any of the six axes.
● Soldier: moves 1 square along any of the six axes.
┃ Lancer: slides any distance along one axis until blocked.
◇ Dia: slides along 3D diagonals until blocked.
✚ Ranger: jumps 1 axis step plus 1 diagonal step. It can jump over pieces.

Captured pieces: captured non-Core pieces go to your piece stand. Blue drops on Layer 2; Red drops on Layer 3.
